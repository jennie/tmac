How to find TMAC

Transit

501 Queen streetcar: Get off at Abell Street, just east of Gladstone. Head south through Lisgar Park.

504 King streetcar: Get off at Sudbury Street, and walk north/west along Sudbury to Lisgar Street. Walk up Lisgar Street to the front of the building.

Parking

There is ample paid parking nearby, including a Green P lot in the building, a lot accessible from the alley between Dovercourt and Lisgar off Sudbury, and street parking on both Lisgar Street and Abell Street.

Address

32 Lisgar Street, Toronto, ON, M6J 0C9 (Google Maps)

Entrance

The main entrance can be a bit tricky to find your first time here. It is not directly off Lisgar, but on the north side of the building facing the park. Our doors are east of the main condo entrance for 36 Lisgar.

From Sudbury and Lisgar, head towards Queen Street until you are at the front of the condo building.

Lost? Call or text (647) 689-6005 and someone will be happy to assist.
